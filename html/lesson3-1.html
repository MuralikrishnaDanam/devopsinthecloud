3.1 - AWS Management Console

Learning Objectives

By the end of this lesson you will be able to -

  • Access the AWS Management Console.

Navigate the AWS Management Console

Tour the AWS Management Console.

  • and go to the AWS Management Console at http://aws.amazon.com/console/.
  • Click on the list of links on the left panel of the EC2 page at https://console.aws.amazon.com/ec2/. Select the Launch Instance button and click Cancel. Select the Security Groups and Load Balancer links.
  • Click on the CloudWatch (https://console.aws.amazon.com/cloudwatch/) tab. Click the Create Alarm button, then Cancel.
  • Click on the Route 53 (https://console.aws.amazon.com/route53/) tab and read through the Getting Started page.
  • Click on the S3 (https://console.aws.amazon.com/s3/) tab.
  • Click on the CloudFormation (https://console.aws.amazon.com/CloudFormation/) tab. Click the Create New Stack button, then click Cancel.
  • Click on the Elastic Beanstalk (https://console.aws.amazon.com/elasticbeanstalk/) tab.
